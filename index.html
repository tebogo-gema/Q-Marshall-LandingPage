About Us

Q'Marshall is more than just a project; it's a reflection of our passion for improving urban transportation in Johannesburg. Inspired by our own experiences navigating the city's taxi network, we set out to create a solution that makes commuting easier and more efficient for everyone.

Our journey began as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where we honed our skills in web development and teamwork. As a diverse team of developers, designers, and transportation enthusiasts, we combined our talents to bring Q'Marshall to life.
